What is the manufacturers obligation?

  • Provide the declaration of conformity UE  and affix the CE marking
  • Provide machine instructions  and technical documentation, including source code and allow users to access them as many times as they want
  • Provide information for machine identification (designation, type or series, year of construction, serial number )
  • Provide the manufacturer information ( name, trademark, mailing address, web address, email )
  • Instructions can be provided in digital format
  • Have the documentation last 10 years

What is the obligation of the representative?

  • Maintain the EU declaration of conformity and the technical documentation for at least 10 years

What is the importers obligation?

  • Ensure that the machine is accompanied by the EU declaration of conformity,  all the technical documentation and the CE marking
  • Ensure that the machine is accompanied by the required documents
  • Ensure that the manufacturer has included the machine and the manufacturers
  • Ensure that the machine is accompanied by information for use
  • Attach the identification of the importer ( postal address, web address and mail )
  • Inform the authorities when the machine presents a risk to the health and safety of people
  • Withdrawal or recovery ; of products that do not comply with essential health and safety requirements
  • Keep a copy of the EU Declaration of Conformity during 10 years

What is the obligation of the distributor?

  • Check that the machine bears the CE marking y the EU declaration of conformity
  • Check that the machine is accompanied by the instruction manual or information for use and the identification data of the manufacturer e importer
  • Withdrawal or recovery of products which do not comply with the essential health and safety requirements
  • Inform the manufacturer or importer (and to the competent authorities) where the machinery presents a risk to human health and safety
