Success cases: Koni

Traceability of railway dampers and optimization of incident reporting

KONI is a recognized leader in the manufacture of high performance shock absorbers and suspension solutions for various sectors, from racing cars to everyday vehicles. With a strong reputation for quality and innovative technology, KONI has faced the challenge of efficiently managing the technical documentation, maintenance and traceability of its products. In search of a comprehensive solution, KONI has turned to TicTAP, transforming the way they manage their processes, improving efficiency, quality and customer satisfaction.

We provide efficient and accurate management of technical documentation

By incorporating TicTAP NFC tags into its assets, KONI has achieved efficient and accurate management of technical documentation for each specific serial number. In addition, TicTAP has provided a complete maintenance module covering installation, overhaul, inspection and other key aspects. It has also enabled the generation of shock absorber certificates, such as the 3.0 and welding inspection reports, ensuring quality and compliance with standards.

We promote efficiency in asset review processes

In short, the implementation of TicTAP has revolutionized the management of KONI, allowing them to have fast and accurate access to technical documentation, detailed monitoring of maintenance and review processes, as well as tools for incident reporting and graphical display of important data. This solution has boosted KONI’s efficiency, traceability and operational success in the shock absorber industry.
